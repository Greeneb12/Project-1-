Newly Released

Create Your Vision

Art Filters enhance your creativity...

Art Filters expand your photographic expression. Three new Art Filters, Vintage, Partial Colour and Shade have been added to the Pen. Shade Effect has also been added to Art Effects for adding bands of shadow on the left and right (or top and bottom) to makes the image spread outward. With these features, the possibilities for expression are endless.

The Vintage Art Filter reproduces the effects that occur on aging film prints, such as colour changes and darkening, to allow a normal everyday scene to be turned into a nostalgic moment.

The Partial Colour Art Filter allows you to keep just a single specific colour and turns all others to monochrome to emphasise images a subject. Create impressive images that are uniquely yours.

The Shade Effect will enhance the impact of Art Filters on a image.
