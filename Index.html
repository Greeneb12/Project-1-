OLYMPUS PEN

Olympus was able to reduce both the weight and size of the Olympus Pen. It featured a simple rear-winding mechanism, a D-Zuiko lens for superior photographic quality, and an attractive design that also made the camera extremely easy to use.
The Pen was a compact mix of innovative ideas that triggered the half-frame camera boom of the 1960s and 1970s.
